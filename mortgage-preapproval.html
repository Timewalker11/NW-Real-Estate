Mortgage

Get Pre-Approved Before You Shop

A pre-approval shows sellers you're ready to buy and gives you a clear, realistic budget before you start touring homes.

Why It Matters

Benefits of Pre-Approval

Know Your Budget

Understand what you can realistically borrow before you fall in love with a home.

Move Faster

Skip a step in the offer process since your financing has already been reviewed.

Stand Out to Sellers

A pre-approval letter signals to sellers that you're a serious, qualified buyer.

The Process

How Pre-Approval Works

  1. Share Your Financial Info

    Provide basic income, asset, and credit information to get the process started.

  2. We Review Your Credit & Income

    Your information is reviewed to determine what loan programs and amounts you may qualify for.

  3. Receive Your Pre-Approval Letter

    Get a letter you can share with your agent and use when making offers.

  4. Start Touring with Confidence

    Shop within your approved range knowing your financing is already lined up.

Get Ready

Documents to Have on Hand

Income & Employment

Recent pay stubs, W-2s or 1099s, and employer contact information.

Assets

Recent bank and investment account statements.
