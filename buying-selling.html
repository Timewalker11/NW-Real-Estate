Buying & Selling Guide

A Clear Path, Whether You're Buying or Selling

Every transaction is different, but the process is easier to navigate when you know what to expect. Here's how we approach each side.

For Buyers

How Buying a Home Works

From your first conversation to closing day, here's a general outline of the buying process.

  1. Get Pre-Approved

    Understand what you can comfortably afford by reviewing financing options and getting pre-approved before you start touring homes.

  2. Define Your Needs

    Talk through your priorities — location, size, budget, and must-haves — so your search stays focused.

  3. Tour Homes

    Visit properties that match your criteria, with feedback along the way to refine your search.

  4. Make an Offer

    When you find the right home, we help you put together a competitive, well-informed offer.

  5. Inspection & Closing

    Coordinate inspections, finalize financing, and walk through closing paperwork until you have the keys.

For First Time Buyers

Buying Your First Home

Purchasing a first home comes with its own set of questions. Here are a few things that often help first time buyers feel more prepared.

Down Payment Assistance

Many first time buyers qualify for down payment assistance programs or loan options with a lower minimum down payment. It's worth asking early what you may be eligible for.

Getting Pre-Approved

A pre-approval gives you a clear budget before you start touring homes and shows sellers you're a serious, qualified buyer.

Understanding the Full Cost

Beyond the purchase price, plan for closing costs, moving expenses, and ongoing costs like property taxes, insurance, and maintenance.

Working With an Agent

Having someone in your corner to explain each step, review paperwork, and answer questions can make the process feel far less overwhelming.

For Sellers

How Selling a Home Works

Selling well takes preparation and a clear marketing plan. Here's a general outline of what that looks like.

  1. Home Valuation

    Review recent comparable sales and current market conditions to help set a realistic, informed listing price.

  2. Prepare & Stage

    Identify simple improvements and staging steps that help your home present well to buyers.

  3. Marketing & Listing

    List the property and get it in front of interested buyers through photos, listing platforms, and outreach.

  4. Offers & Negotiation

    Review incoming offers together and negotiate terms that align with your goals.

  5. Closing

    Coordinate inspections, paperwork, and final details through to a successful closing.

Thinking about buying or selling?

Reach out and we'll talk through where you are in the process and what makes sense next.
