Buying & Selling a Home

Whichever side of the transaction you're on, we'll help you move with confidence.

For Buyers

Your Path to Homeownership

From pre-approval to move-in day, here's what the buying process typically looks like.

1

Get Pre-Approved

Know your budget before you shop. Our in-house lending team can typically issue a pre-approval letter within 24-48 hours.

2

Define Your Wish List

We'll talk through neighborhoods, must-haves, and deal-breakers to build a targeted search.

3

Tour Homes

See listings in person or via video walkthroughs, with your agent's honest read on each property.

4

Make an Offer

We prepare a competitive offer strategy backed by recent comparable sales data.

5

Inspection & Appraisal

We coordinate inspections and negotiate any repairs, while your loan moves through underwriting.

6

Closing Day

Sign your final paperwork, wire your funds, and get the keys to your new home.

For Sellers

Sell for the Best Price, With the Least Stress

Our sellers typically go under contract in under three weeks using this process.

1

Free Home Valuation

We analyze recent sales, current inventory, and your home's condition to recommend a list price.

2

Prepare Your Home

Staging and light-repair recommendations that give you the best return for your investment.

3

Professional Marketing

Photography, floor plans, and listing syndication to the MLS and major home search sites.

4

Showings & Offers

We manage showings, field offers, and advise you on terms beyond just price.

5

Negotiate & Accept

We negotiate on your behalf through inspection contingencies and buyer requests.

6

Close & Get Paid

Sign closing documents and receive your proceeds, typically within 30-45 days of accepting an offer.
